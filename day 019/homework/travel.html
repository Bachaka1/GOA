About Georgia

Georgia is a country located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia. It is famous for its mountains, sea, and delicious food.

Top Destinations

Tbilisi

The capital city known for its beautiful architecture and history.

Kazbegi

Perfect destination for mountain lovers and adventure seekers.

Batumi

Beautiful seaside city on the Black Sea coast.

Why Visit Georgia?

  • Amazing nature
  • Rich culture
  • Friendly people

Travel Tips

  1. Visit in spring or autumn
  2. Try traditional food
  3. Explore local markets
